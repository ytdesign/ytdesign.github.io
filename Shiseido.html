Field of Presence

Selected for NUS Gradbook 2020

 

A case study with Shiseido to design a pop-up installation for the Changi Airport to attract new and existing customers. A part of their new 2020 campaign highlighting the Ultimate product. Worked as a vertically integrated team, alongside Shiseido brand representatives, to develop a brand experience while staying true to its values. Iterated through a cycles of design through prototypes and 3D models.
