about me

I'm an Industrial Design Major graduating from Georgia Tech this year! I'm currently seeking full-time experience design opportunities starting May 2021. If you have any leads, please feel free to reach out via email or shoot me a message on LinkedIn.

I love to listen.

I really enjoy talking to people and listening to their stories and experiences. Through my experiences in customer service from retail, to being a barista trainer at Starbucks, to an event manager at the Historic Academy of Medicine, and more, I strive to empathize with the users and design for their needs.

I've always been interested in problem solving. The field of design doesn't have restrictions to a single answer. The possibilites are endless. As an experience designer, I'm interested in curating unique experiences with products, fulfilling objectives, events, and more without the snags of confusion.As a product designer, I believe strongly in designing for people. I am experienced in a human centered design approach that utilizes testing with the user in a quantitative manner to collect and analyze data, and refining iteratively.
